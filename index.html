Our Products

Virgin Coconut Oil (VCO)

Is coconut oil that is processed and extracted from fresh coconuts using standardized food processing technology, and does not use chemical processes (non-RBD). VCO can be used as a health supplement, various internal medicines, and as a medicine for external diseases such as skin.

Coconut Crude Oil (CCO)

Is crude coconut oil extracted from copra (fresh coconut that has been dried). It can be further processed into RBD (Refined Coconut Oil) coconut oil which can be used in various products such as the beauty, food, and health care industries.

Refined Coconut Oil (RBD)

It is crude coconut oil that has been purified, bleached and deodorized. RBD has its own characteristics due to the high pressure refining process. This coconut oil usually looks clearer, has a bland taste, and does not smell, so it can be easily used as a mixture of raw materials for making soap, cosmetics, and processed foods.

RBD Hydrogenate Coconut Oil (RBD HCNO)

is RBD CNO that has gone through the Hydrogenation process. RBD HCNO is a solid white fat with a clear texture and liquid when applied to heat. RBD HCNO can be used as the main raw material in the food industry.

Coconut Fatty Acid Distilate (CFAD)

is a by-product obtained during the refining process of crude coconut oil. It is a reddish brown liquid at room temperature and is mainly used in the soap making industry.

Copra Meal

Is animal feed with a protein source derived from processing copra into coconut oil. Dried copra is extracted into 70% coconut oil and 30% copra flour which is brown when ripe.

Pellet Copra

Copra pellets are machine-pressed and dried coconut meat or kernels. It is a by-product of the extraction process from coconut oil.
